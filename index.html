About ICT Club

Background

The ICT Club at ZSNHS-SHS helps students explore computing fundamentals through short tutorials followed by hands-on projects, creating a welcoming space for beginners and advanced learners.

Goal

Our goal is to build practical tech skills, encourage creative problem-solving, and prepare members for competitions, internships, and future careers in IT.
